Research and Innovation Page

Welcome to the dynamic realm of Research and Innovation at the University of Science and Technology of Southern Philippines - Cagayan de Oro Campus (USTP CDO). Our commitment to pushing boundaries, fostering creativity, and driving progress is at the heart of our academic ethos. Explore a world of groundbreaking research initiatives led by our esteemed faculty and passionate students. From advancements in technology to innovative solutions addressing real-world challenges, our research endeavors are diverse, impactful, and aimed at shaping a better future.

Innovation Hub & Student-Led Initiatives

Step into our Innovation Hub, where ideas come to life. This collaborative space is a melting pot of creativity, bringing together minds from various disciplines to ideate, create, and innovate. Witness the birth of transformative projects that have the potential to make a lasting impact on industries and communities.

At USTP CDO, we empower our students to be the architects of change. Engage with student-led research projects and innovation competitions that showcase the ingenuity and resourcefulness of the next generation of leaders in science and technology

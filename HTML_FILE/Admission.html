Admission Page

We are thrilled to welcome you to the Admission Page of the University of Science and Technology of Southern Philippines (USTP). Your journey to academic excellence begins here, and we are excited to be a part of your educational adventure. At USTP, we pride ourselves on providing a world-class education that prepares students for success in the ever-evolving landscape of science and technology. Our commitment to innovation, research, and academic excellence sets us apart. Navigate through the diverse range of programs we offer, tailored to meet the demands of today's dynamic industries. From cutting-edge technology to groundbreaking research, our curriculum is designed to empower you with the skills and knowledge needed to make a significant impact in your chosen field.

Admission Process

To begin your journey with us, please fill out our convenient online admission form. This form is designed to gather essential information to better understand your educational background and aspirations. Once you've submitted the form, our dedicated admission team will carefully review your application. Upon receiving your completed online form, you can expect an email from us with further instructions and details regarding the next steps of the admission process. This email will include important information about additional documents required, any entrance exams if applicable, and guidance on how to proceed with the next stages. We value your interest in our institution and are committed to making the admission process as smooth as possible. Should you have any questions or need assistance at any point, feel free to reach out to our admission team. We're here to support you on your academic journey.
